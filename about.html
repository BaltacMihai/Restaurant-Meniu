The story of the Torotoro tribe

Orotoro is the name of an ancient hunting tribe. A tribe from the time when people were hunter-gatherers. It was known among Homo sapiens and Neanderthals as the most skilled at hunting. So clever that they always had enough meat, which is why its members gave up picking fruits and vegetables.



It is said that the Tortorians tamed the fire just to cook the meat, deciphering its mysteries so that it would get a crispy crust on the outside, but be juicy on the inside, complemented by the aroma of smoke.
